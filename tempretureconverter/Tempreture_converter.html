Conversion Formulas

Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Celsius ↔ Kelvin

K = °C + 273.15

°C = K - 273.15

Fahrenheit ↔ Kelvin

K = (°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15

°F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
